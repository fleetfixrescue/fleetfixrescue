About Us

Fleet Fix Rescue believes every mile matters. Trucks are more than machines. They are the backbone of America’s economy. From long-haul semi trucks crossing state lines to dump trucks and heavy-duty vehicles supporting local work, we understand how important it is to keep trucks moving.

That belief shapes our mission: to provide reliable, high-quality, and dependable truck rescue services across the nation.

Our Journey

Fleet Fix Rescue was built on a simple belief. Truck drivers deserve more than just a tow truck. They need dependable support when it matters most. Being stranded on the roadside, far from home, with pressure building and schedules slipping is something we understand well.

That is why we created Fleet Fix Rescue. To be the team that responds at any hour, with skilled technicians, the right tools, and a genuine commitment to helping drivers move forward.

Our Services

We offer complete rescue and roadside solutions for all types of commercial trucks, including:

  • Emergency towing and recovery for semi trucks, tractor trailers, and freight vehicles
  • Mobile repair and mechanic services for fast, on-site breakdown assistance
  • Cargo support services, including loading, unloading, and transport coordination
  • Route assistance and planning to help drivers recover from unexpected delays
  • Support for all truck types, from long-haul rigs to local dump and work trucks

Why Fleet Fix Rescue

What sets Fleet Fix Rescue apart is not just our equipment or experience. It is our commitment to truckers. We respect the responsibility drivers carry and the importance of the cargo they move every day.

When you contact us, you are not just asking for help. You are connecting with a team that understands the road, the pressure, and the urgency.

Our Commitment

No matter where you are in the United States, our promise remains the same: fast response, professional service, and reliable support.

Fleet Fix Rescue keeping America’s trucks moving.
